# AdVantage

**Marketing team at your fingertips.**

Built for the [NEAR AI Agents That Act Hackathon](https://github.com/arora13/AdVantageV1).

Paste a product or business idea, and a **5-agent swarm** researches your market, finds contacts, writes launch copy, and stages everything in a **marketing dashboard**.
